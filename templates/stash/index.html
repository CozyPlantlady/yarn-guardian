What is Yarn Guardian?

Yarn Guardian is a little helper, that helps you keep record of your yarns.

What can Yarn Guardian do?

You can log a yarn and give it's name, producer, color and material

You can make notes too!

You can tag yarn as a favorite to find it easier

If you ever have to undo a project you can tag yarn as frogged

And that's not all!

You can start a new project and keep notes about that as well.
You can add an URL to another page to have quick access to a pattern,
and tag a yarn you want to use with it (currently only one yarn though).
